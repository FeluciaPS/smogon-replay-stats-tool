# Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Why is replay loading so slow/can't you make it faster?**

PS! limits how quickly you can download replays to about once every 200-250ms, there's nothing I can do to speed that up more than I already have. A typical 1v1 tournament thread has anywhere between like 90 and 180 replays (bo5 moment), especially dense threads (like world cup pools) can have like 576 matchups with 3-7 replays each (if my math is right that's 1944 to 3312 replays). Multiply that by 250ms and you get almost 15 minutes of loading... Sorry!
